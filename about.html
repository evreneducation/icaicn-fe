About the Conference

About ICAICN 2026

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence & Communication Networks (ICAICN 2026)


Register Now

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence & Communication Networks (ICAICN 2026) aims to bring together leading researchers, academicians, industry experts, and students to explore advancements and innovations in Artificial Intelligence and Communication Networks. The conference provides a dynamic platform for presenting cutting-edge research, fostering collaboration, and discussing future directions in intelligent systems and next-generation communication technologies.

ICAICN 2026 will feature keynote speeches, technical sessions, workshops, and panel discussions covering a wide range of topics including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, wireless communication, network security, and more. The conference encourages interdisciplinary research and collaboration to address the challenges and opportunities in the rapidly evolving fields of AI and communication networks.
