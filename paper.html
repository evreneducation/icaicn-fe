SUBMIT YOUR PAPERS

Paper Submission Guidelines

Submission Format

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research work in the form of full papers (8-15 pages) in DOCX/PDF/LaTeX format (less than 10MB).

Submission Portal

Submissions should be made through the official conference submission portal

https://ICAICN.TRAVEON.in/paper.html

Review Process

All submissions will undergo a rigorous peer review process by an expert panel. Papers will be evaluated based on originality, significance and quality.

Paper Presentation

Accepted papers must be presented at the conference by at least one of the authors.

Unpublished Article

Only unpublished articles will be considered as valid for submission.

Plagiarism

Plagiarism of each individual article should be less than 15% and the single source similarity index should not exceed 5%. However, the final decision of publication is up to the Journal Editor's decision.

Copyright Forms

Signed copy of copyright form(s) to be submitted by the author wherever required.

Individual Articles

Individual articles must not go beyond 20 pages in the publisher template.


Quality Images

Good quality images, typed equations, editable tables and proper citations to the references are expected in the papers.
