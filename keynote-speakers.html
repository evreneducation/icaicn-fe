Keynote Speakers

D. Virmani
GGSIP University, India

Dr. Deepali Virmani is a distinguished academic leader with over two decades of experience in Computer Science and Information Technology. She earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Delhi and currently serves as Professor and Head of the Guru Tegh Bahadur Innovation and Research Centre at GTBIT. Prior to this, she held senior academic leadership positions at the Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies and Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology, where she strengthened research culture and industry collaboration. Dr. Virmani's scholarly work covers Communication Networks, Data Science, Knowledge Engineering, Sensor Networks, and Artificial Intelligence. She has published 150+ research papers, accumulating 4,100+ citations, and has mentored the next generation of researchers by guiding 100+ B.Tech. projects and supervising multiple Ph.D. scholars. Her contributions have been recognized through several honors, including the Best Researcher Award and Excellence in Research Award. Committed to community service in scholarship, Dr. Virmani serves on editorial boards of international journals and is an Associate Editor with Open Computer Science (De Gruyter) and Springer Nature. She is also a frequent reviewer for leading IEEE, Elsevier, and Springer journals. As an ICAICN keynote speaker, she brings a strong blend of research rigor, leadership, and real-world impact.

Dr. Nabeela Hasan
New Delhi Institute of Management, New Delhi

Dr. Nabeela Hasan is an accomplished academic and researcher currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at New Delhi Institute of Management, New Delhi. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Jamia Millia Islamia and has a strong academic background with MCA and BCA degrees. With extensive research experience, she has published numerous papers in reputed Scopus and Web of Science indexed journals and conferences, focusing on areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Industrial IoT, and Cloud Computing. She is also a recipient of prestigious fellowships like UGC-NET JRF/SRF and has earned recognition through awards for her research contributions. In addition to her teaching and research, she actively contributes to academic administration, mentoring students, reviewing international journals, and delivering expert lectures, demonstrating her commitment to advancing education and innovation in the field of computer science.

Shruthi S.V.
Dayananda Sagar University-Online , Bangalore, Karnataka

Shruthi S V is a dedicated academician and researcher currently serving as an Assistant Professor and Course Coordinator at Dayananda Sagar University (Online), Bangalore. She holds an M.Sc. in Computer Science, B.Ed., and has qualified KSET, while pursuing her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Mysore University. With over a decade of teaching experience across reputed institutions, she has contributed extensively to curriculum development, online education, and student mentorship. Her expertise spans programming, artificial intelligence, data science, and computer networks, along with active involvement in academic leadership roles such as project coordination, virtual labs, and institutional committees. She has an impressive portfolio of research publications, patents, books, and conference contributions, along with multiple awards and recognitions for excellence in teaching and research. Additionally, she actively participates as a reviewer, editor, speaker, and international academic contributor, reflecting her strong commitment to innovation and quality education.

Dr Vanita Kshirsagar
Dr D Y Patil Institute of Technology, Pimpri

Dr. Prashant Johri is a distinguished academician and researcher, currently serving as Professor in the School of Computing Science and Engineering at Galgotias University, Greater Noida, where he also leads the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Research Group under the Intel Center of Excellence. He holds a B.Sc. (Hons.) and MCA from Aligarh Muslim University and earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Jiwaji University, Gwalior. With over two decades of academic and administrative experience, including his tenure as Professor and Director of MCA programs at Galgotias Institute of Management & Technology (GIMT) and Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology (NIET), Dr. Johri has significantly contributed to teaching, research, and institutional leadership. His research spans Artificial Intelligence, Communication Networks, Blockchain, Software Reliability, Big Data Security, Cloud Computing, Image Processing, and Information Security, with applications across healthcare, agriculture, and intelligent systems. He has published more than 100 research papers in reputed journals and conferences and has edited volumes such as Applications of Communication Networks (Springer) and Trends and Advancements of Image Processing and Its Applications (IGI Global). A sought-after keynote speaker and reviewer, Dr. Johri continues to mentor young researchers and advance cutting-edge innovations in computer science and engineering.
